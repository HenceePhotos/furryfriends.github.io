About us


We started this business because there wasn't really any high end dog groomers around. We wanted to be the first so we set up this business! We want to give your pooch the best groom of their life using high quality products and tools.


We offer the highest qualities of dog grooming. We utilise the best products such as shampoos and conditioners to make sure your pooch stays fresh for longer. We also have a pick up and drop off service which is included in the groom price!


We only have the BEST staff Ireland can offer. All our groomers are fully trained and certified!
